The History

Multi agriculture farming has its roots in traditional agricultural practices that were developed by early farming communities around the world. These communities relied on the land to provide them with food, fiber, and other resources, and they developed sustainable farming practices that allowed them to maintain the productivity of the land over long periods of time.
